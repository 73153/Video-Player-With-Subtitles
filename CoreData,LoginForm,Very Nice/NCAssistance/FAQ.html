How safe is NCA?
Not safe enough to stand a hacker attack, but enough to protect your information against someone who has your phone in hand.
What if I entered my access code wrong 10 times on the login screen?
NCA will be locked for 24 hours. 1 attempt is allowed every 24 hours thereafter.
Will there be a newer version?
Perhaps. If you have any requirements, feel free to let us know.
