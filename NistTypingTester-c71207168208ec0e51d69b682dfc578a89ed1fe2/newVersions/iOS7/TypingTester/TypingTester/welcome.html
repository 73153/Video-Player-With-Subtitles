Welcome to the NIST study on text and password entry on mobile devices.

In this part of the study you will type short text phrases.

You will see one phrase per screen.

Please type each phrase as quickly and as accurately as you can.

After typing a phrase, press the '>' button to move on to the next phrase.

The button will be located in the upper right corner of the screen on an iPhone or iPod touch, and below the text box on an iPad.

After you finish typing the phrases, you will see an instructions screen for the next part of the study.
